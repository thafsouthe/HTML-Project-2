Delicious Bites Menu

Appetizers

  • Chorba Frick (Soup)

    Traditional soup made with cracked green wheat, vegetables, meat (lamb or chicken), spices, and fresh herbs.

  • Borek (Burek)

    A crispy pastry filled with spiced minced meat (usually lamb or beef), onions, parsley, or cheese, usually fried or baked.

  • Salade Mechouia

    A refreshing salad made from grilled vegetables (peppers, tomatoes, onions, and garlic) mixed with olive oil and vinegar, served cold.

Main Courses

  • Couscous

    The most famous Algerian dish, couscous is steamed semolina served with a hearty stew of lamb, chicken, or vegetables, topped with chickpeas, raisins, and a flavorful sauce.

  • Rechta

    A type of Algerian pasta served with a rich chicken or lamb stew. The dish is flavored with saffron and garnished with chickpeas and a variety of vegetables.

Desserts

  • Baklava

    A sweet pastry made of thin layers of phyllo dough, filled with chopped nuts (usually Nuts or almonds), and soaked in honey syrup or orange blossom water.

  • Kalb el Louz

    A semolina cake made with almonds, sugar, and a syrup made from honey and rosewater, typically served at festive occasions.

  • Basbousa

    A semolina cake soaked in simple syrup or rose water. It's usually garnished with almonds and has a light, fluffy texture.
